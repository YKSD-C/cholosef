The purpose of my Advocacy

The purpose of the "Engineering for Disaster Resilience" advocacy is to promote the development and implementation of
disaster-resilient infrastructure and technologies in disaster-prone countries like the Philippines and Japan.
Countries like the Philippines and Japan are frequently affected by natural disasters due to their geographic location.
Both nations face significant risks from seismic activity, tropical storms, and coastal flooding, which result in devastating impacts
on human life, infrastructure, and the economy.
